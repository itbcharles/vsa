Game Rules

Official rules for Senior Assassin. Violations may result in disqualification.

1. Registration

Only verified seniors may participate. Accounts will be approved after verification and after the $5 entry fee is paid. The last day to register is April 27 at 11:59 PM PST.

Payment accepted via Cash, Venmo, or CashApp. Message the Instagram account (@vapaseniorassassin) for payment instructions.

If you forgot your password, message the account for help.

3. Safe Zones

Eliminations are not allowed inside school buildings, religious buildings, or during prom and graduation events.

4. Approved Weapons

Only the following are allowed: spray bottles, water guns (non-motorized), and water balloons. No other items may be used.

No physical contact is allowed at any time.

5. Goggle Rule

Wearing goggles protects you from elimination. Goggles must be securely worn on the head and may not be altered in any way.

6. Elimination Rules

All eliminations must be filmed and submitted to the Instagram account. No video = no kill.

Eliminations must be reported within 24 hours to count.

7. Target Updates

New targets are assigned every Wednesday and Saturday (midday).

8. Prizes

Winnings are split among the top three players:

  • 1st Place: 60%
  • 2nd Place: 30%
  • 3rd Place: 10%
